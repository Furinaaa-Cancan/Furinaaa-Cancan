### About

Graduate researcher focusing on **large-scale population health databases** and **nutritional data analysis**.
My work centers on extracting predictive and phenotypic structure from harmonized cohort data — combining supervised
machine learning with unsupervised clustering to identify health-relevant subpopulations and risk patterns.

---

### Research Interests

#### Population Health Cohorts

Building reproducible ML pipelines on internationally harmonized cohort databases:

| Database | Region | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **NHANES** | United States | Cross-sectional health & nutrition surveillance |
| **KNHANES** | South Korea | Korean national health & nutrition survey |
| **UKB** (UK Biobank) | United Kingdom | Deep phenotyping, biomarkers, imaging, genomics |
| **CHARLS** | China | Middle-aged & older adults longitudinal study |
| **ELSA** | England | English longitudinal study of ageing |
| **ELSA-Brasil** | Brazil | Adult health longitudinal study |

**Core methods**: machine-learning risk prediction · unsupervised phenotype clustering · trajectory & latent-class modeling.

#### Nutritional Data Analysis

Linking dietary intake to health outcomes through curated food-composition resources:

| Database | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **FNDDS** | Food & Nutrient Database for Dietary Studies — recipe-level nutrient mapping |
| **PE / Phenol-Explorer** | Polyphenol composition of foods |
| **USDA FoodData Central** | Standard reference & branded food composition |

**Applications**: dietary pattern derivation · polyphenol exposure assessment · diet-disease association modeling.
